# Favorite — Rollout Playbook (Vertical by Vertical)

How the platform rolls out across **verticals** and, within each vertical, across **countries**. Two nested loops: the vertical sequence is the top level; the per-country launch repeats inside each vertical. Guiding rules: **no country goes live without a secured prize**, **qualification is earned, never given**, and the contest is **free to enter and free to win** throughout.

---

## 1. Top-Level Vertical Sequence

Launch easiest + most monetizable first, hardest last.

| Order | Vertical | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Pets — Dogs** | Launch vertical. Lightest legal/moderation load, deepest sponsors, huge supply. |
| 1 | **Pets — Cats** | Second pet competition, same engine, run in parallel. |
| 2 | **Cars / builds** | Highest ad RPM and strongest evergreen SEO; consider accelerating ahead of gardens. |
| 3 | **Gardens** | Very low moderation, decent value. |
| 4 | **Couples** | Last — lowest ad value, heaviest moderation/privacy. Launch only once platform + moderation are mature. |

**Gate to add the next vertical:** the current vertical's loop is proven in at least one country (pre-reg -> sponsor -> qualification -> live -> fulfilment -> retention), fraud is controlled, and moderation is stable. Verticals are config on a category-aware engine, so adding one is setup, not a rebuild — but don't open a new vertical until the prior one's flywheel is turning.

---

## 2. Per-Vertical, Per-Country Phases

This loop repeats for every (vertical, country) pair — starting with (Pets/Dogs, Country #1) and (Pets/Cats, Country #1).

### Phase 0 — Pre-registration (continuous)
- Contestants join the country+vertical waitlist, create a profile, build their story.
- Each receives a **unique referral code** (see §4).
- Keep them *active*: profile-building, story posts, inviting other contestants. No end date until activation.

### Phase 1 — Sponsor secured (activation gate)
- A sponsor commits the first prize. **Prefer in-kind** so the company touches no money/goods. Pet/car sponsors are the easiest first calls.
- **Fallback (define up front):** if no sponsor within a set window (e.g. 60 days after a minimum waitlist size), launch with a modest **company-funded gift-card prize**. Never let a country stall indefinitely.
- Announce the prize/sponsor — a launch moment.

### Phase 2 — Qualification window (knockout)
- Opens on activation, fixed window (e.g. 14 days). Contestants qualify via a path in §4.
- Non-qualifiers stay on the waitlist for next round.
- **Fairness rule:** the live round starts only when this window closes; all qualifiers enter together.

### Phase 3 — Live competition (monthly)
- Free daily votes; each contestant has its one free 24h 5x boost to activate when it chooses. No payment affects ranking.
- Prize and growing-prize tiers displayed. Daily social content (§7).

### Phase 4 — Winner & prize fulfilment
- Winner declared; identity verified; prize delivered (sponsor in-kind, or company gift-card/voucher/travel code). Cash deferred until payout rails exist.
- Publicize winner + sponsor thank-you — your best content and proof for the next sponsor.

### Phase 5 — Retain & repeat
- Vertical+country runs monthly. Non-winners roll forward; new contestants keep qualifying.
- Finished entries convert to **permanent evergreen story pages** (SEO base).
- A self-sustaining (vertical, country) becomes the template for the next country, then the next vertical.

## 3. Two Ways to Grow From a Proven Loop

Once (Pets/Dogs, Country #1) works, you can expand along two axes:

- **Across countries (same vertical):** open Pets in Country #2, #3 … driven by sponsor pipeline.
- **Across verticals (proven engine):** open Cars, then Gardens, then Couples — each repeating the per-country loop.

Sequence by where sponsors are warmest. A secured sponsor is the real unlock for each (vertical, country).

---

## 4. How a Contestant Qualifies (three paths)

Any one path qualifies; multiple paths mean no contestant is ever stuck.

### Path A — Referral (primary)
- Qualify by getting **one other contestant** to register and verify with your referral code.
- **The chain:** the referred contestant is then in the same position and qualifies by referring one more. Each link qualifies itself by extending the chain.
- *Example:* A refers B -> A qualified; B refers C -> B qualified; C refers D -> C qualified.
- **Anti-fraud:** the referral counts only once the referred contestant is email-verified with a complete profile (story + at least one photo) — stops fake entries created just to self-qualify.

### Path B — Vote knockout (open fallback)
- Reach the **minimum vote threshold** during the qualification window. Always open, so chains can't trap their last members.

### Path C — Founders' bonus (first cohort only, capped)
- For a (vertical, country)'s first activation, the earliest **N** profile-complete pre-registrants auto-qualify as a thank-you. Strictly capped; inaugural round only.

## 5. Gates & Guardrails

| Risk | Guardrail |
|---|---|
| Country stalls with no sponsor | Company-funded gift-card fallback after a deadline. |
| Waitlist floods with dead profiles | Qualification is earned (referral/votes); founders' bonus capped. |
| Contestants leak during the waiting gap | Keep them active: profiles, story posts, referrals, a "most anticipated" list. |
| Latecomers feel behind | Live round starts only after qualification closes — all enter together. |
| Referral fraud | Referred contestant must be verified + profile-complete; flag shared device/IP clusters. |
| Opening a vertical too early | Gate: prior vertical's loop proven and moderation stable first. |
| Spreading thin | Sponsor pipeline drives both country and vertical sequencing. |

---

## 6. Sequencing Strategy

- **Prove one (vertical, country) fully** — (Pets/Dogs + Pets/Cats, Country #1) — before scaling either axis.
- **Expand by sponsor warmth, not market size.**
- **Each (vertical, country) needs its own legal check** before activation; reflect in published terms. *(Operational playbook, not legal advice.)*
- **Cars is the highest-RPM vertical** — once pets is proven, accelerating cars over gardens compounds revenue faster.

## 7. Social Media Playbook

Amplifies the referral loop and drives contestants' audiences back to the platform.

### Accounts
- Start with **one main brand account** per platform (Instagram, Facebook, TikTok). Consider per-vertical or per-country handles only once each justifies it; don't fragment early.

### Content tied to phases
- **Pre-reg:** countdowns, "this country/vertical is filling up," waitlist milestones, calls to invite another contestant.
- **Sponsor reveal:** prize announcement; celebrate the sponsor.
- **Qualification:** "qualification open," "X qualified so far," "closes in 3 days."
- **Live:** **daily contestant spotlights** (the single most important recurring post — sends each contestant's followers back to vote), leaderboard updates, prize-tier unlocks, "voting closes soon."
- **Winner:** reveal + sponsor thank-you; proof for the next sponsor.

### Cadence & loop
- Daily during a live competition; a few times a week otherwise. Reshare contestants' own posts. Every contestant posts with a branded hashtag + referral link, so social distribution and the referral chain feed each other.

## 8. Per-(Vertical, Country) Launch Checklist

1. [ ] Open pre-registration (always-on).
2. [ ] Reach a minimum waitlist size that makes a sponsor pitch credible.
3. [ ] Complete the legal check; finalize competition terms.
4. [ ] Secure a sponsor (prefer in-kind) or trigger the gift-card fallback.
5. [ ] Announce the prize/sponsor.
6. [ ] Open the qualification window (referral + votes; founders' bonus if first round).
7. [ ] Run qualification-phase social content.
8. [ ] Close qualification; lock the qualified field.
9. [ ] Start the live competition; daily spotlights + milestone posts.
10. [ ] Declare winner; verify identity; fulfil prize.
11. [ ] Convert finished entries to evergreen story pages.
12. [ ] Post winner + sponsor thank-you; roll into next round.

---

## 9. Risks & Notes

- **Referral fraud** is the main model-specific risk — verification + complete-profile before counting; cluster flagging.
- **Keep the vote-knockout path always open.**
- **Privacy/GDPR** — heaviest for couples (launched last for this reason), but applies throughout.
- **Moderation protects revenue** — AdSense pulls monetization from UGC platforms allowing explicit content.
- Operational playbook, not legal advice. Confirm prize-competition rules per (vertical, country) before activating.
